- Kratos, once a god, is now haunted by nightmares and shunned by other gods.
- He joins the Spartan army and battles a giant statue, losing most of his powers in the process.
- Zeus kills him, but Gaia saves him and sends him to find the Sisters of Fate to change his fate.
- Kratos encounters various enemies, including Titans, and eventually reaches the Sisters of Fate.
- He kills two of the sisters and acquires the Loom of Fate to return to the moment Zeus betrayed him.
- Kratos battles Zeus, nearly defeats him, but Athena sacrifices herself, revealing Kratos is Zeus' son.
- Kratos travels back in time and rescues the Titans, leading them to Mount Olympus to confront the gods.
