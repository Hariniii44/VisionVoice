Bill Gates, co-founder of Microsoft, is renowned not only for his technological innovations but also for his philanthropic endeavors. Born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, Gates displayed an early aptitude for computer programming. Alongside Paul Allen, he established Microsoft in 1975, revolutionizing the personal computing industry with the development of the Windows operating system. Over the years, Gates transitioned from leading Microsoft to focusing on charitable initiatives through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. Through his foundation, Gates has dedicated his resources to addressing global health crises, poverty alleviation, and education reform, leaving an indelible mark on both the tech industry and the world at large
